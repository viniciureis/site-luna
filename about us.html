The Reboot Comic Store is a bookstore specializing in comics and manga and other nerd and geek items.

Since 2012, we have brought to the local market a large and updated collection, available in two well-located stores in Fortaleza/CE.

We also have a virtual store, aiming to facilitate access to our collection for customers from other cities and states.

Our goal

Create an environment of socialization and entertainment for our customers, in addition to promoting the comics market in our city and state,

meeting the needs of the market through our always updated collection and a large stock for prompt
delivery, as well as rescuing human contact.

in relationships, enabling the meeting and development of geek and nerd culture, through actions such as launches and other events.

With a specialized team, we intend to help you enter this magical universe of the ninth art and keep your collection always up to date!
